Cepelinai

Ingredients

  • 12 Uncooked potatoes
  • 3 Baked potatoes
  • Pinch of Salt

For filling

  • 500g Ground pork
  • 1 Onion
  • Pinch of Salt
  • Pinch of Peper

For sauce

  • 1 Onion
  • 2 tablespoons Fat
  • 100g Bacon
  • 4 tablespoons Sour cream

Preparation

  1. Peeled potatoes are grated and strained

  2. The liquid is not poured out, but it is waited until the starch settles. Then the liquid is drained, and the starch is added to the grated potatos.

  3. Boiled potatoes are crushed and mixed with raw potato grater, starch, salted, and kneaded well.

  4. Chopped onion is fried for the filling. Minced meat is mixed with fried onion, salt and pepper.

  5. Approximately 80 g of dough is taken, flattened, the filling is placed, the flat sheet is folded, the edges are well pressed and oblong dumplings are made.

  6. They are placed in boiling salted water and boiled for 20-25 minutes, stirring carefully.

  7. For the sauce, bacon peaces are fried in fat for a few minutes, then chopped onions are added, fried for a few more minutes, then sour cream is added.

  8. Scooped out of the water, cepelinai are placed in a heated half-bowl and poured with sauce.
